RP fat loss guidance
Cut volume strategy

Keep volume at low-to-mid MAV. Cutting below MEV risks muscle loss; pushing near MRV adds fatigue that compounds with caloric deficit. Prioritize high SFR (stimulus-to-fatigue ratio) exercises.

Reduce total weekly sets by 10–20% vs. a bulk phase. Keep MEV minimums for priority muscles.
